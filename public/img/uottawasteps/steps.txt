Step 1: http://www.uottawa.ca/en/students
Step 2: Click on Rabaska-On line registration
Step 3: Click "My Timetable"
Step 4: Select a week that falls into the semester that you want the calendar for and contains all the courses that you're taking in the week.
For example: The schedule for the week of September 20th would fall in the fall semester.
The sechedule for the week of January 20th would fall in the winter semester.
Step 5: Right click on page, click "Save as..." and be sure "Webpage, Complete" is selected and click save to the location you want to save it to.
Step 6: Upload the Rabaska.html you just saved and output a beautiful ICS!

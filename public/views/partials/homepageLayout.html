  • Add Calendar to Google Calendar
    • Step 1 - Open Google Calendar website, Be sure you are logged into a Gmail Account
    Step 2 - Just to the right of "Other calendars" on the left sidebar of your Google Calendar you will see a downwards facing arrow. Click on this arrow and then select "Import calendar"
    Step 3 - Find your recently saved calendar file and select or create a new Calendar to drop all your dates into
  • Add Calendar to Devices
    • For Mac-Book : Just Double click and open the calendar file generate by this site (.ics file), import to Calendar App
    For IPhone : Send the calendar file generate by this site (.ics file) to IPhone via email, and open the file, import to IPhone Calendar
    For Android: Download Google Calendar from Google Play, send the calendar file generate by this site (.ics file) to Android via email, and open the file with Google Calendar
